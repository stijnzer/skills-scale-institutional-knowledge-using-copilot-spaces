# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## QA Engineer

### Role Summary
QA Engineers design, implement, and execute tests to validate project quality. They collaborate with Product Managers and Developers to define acceptance criteria, testing strategies, and ensure smooth bug triage and release readiness.

### Responsibilities
- Design and execute test plans for automated and manual testing
- Collaborate with PMs and Developers to define acceptance criteria and testing strategy
- Conduct bug triage and track defect resolution
- Validate release readiness and quality gates
- Maintain test coverage and test automation frameworks
- Perform regression, integration, and acceptance testing

### Goals
- Ensure high product quality and reliability
- Reduce defect escape rate to production
- Improve test automation coverage and efficiency
- Enable fast and confident releases

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups to report testing progress and blockers
- Sprint planning to estimate testing effort
- Bug reports and test case documentation
- Quality status reports to PMs and stakeholders

---

## UX Designer

### Role Summary
UX Designers define and advocate for user experience principles, ensuring the product is intuitive and usable. They create wireframes, conduct user research and usability tests, and provide design feedback to the team.

### Responsibilities
- Design wireframes, mockups, and interactive prototypes
- Conduct user research and usability testing
- Define and maintain design systems and patterns
- Collaborate with Product Managers on user requirements
- Work with Developers on design handoff and implementation
- Gather and incorporate stakeholder and user feedback
- Advocate for accessibility and inclusive design

### Goals
- Deliver intuitive and user-friendly experiences
- Ensure design consistency across the product
- Validate designs through user research and testing
- Reduce user friction and improve satisfaction

### Typical Communication
- Design reviews and feedback sessions
- User research findings and recommendations
- Design specifications and handoff documentation
- Collaboration sessions with PMs and Developers

---

## Security Specialist

### Role Summary
Security Specialists assess risks, define mitigation strategies, review code and infrastructure for vulnerabilities, and guide compliance efforts. They ensure the product meets security standards and protects user data.

### Responsibilities
- Conduct security assessments and threat modeling
- Review code and infrastructure for security vulnerabilities
- Define and implement security policies and controls
- Guide compliance efforts and security best practices
- Respond to security incidents and coordinate remediation
- Collaborate with Developers on secure coding practices
- Work with Operations on secure deployment and monitoring

### Goals
- Minimize security vulnerabilities and attack surface
- Ensure compliance with security standards and regulations
- Build security awareness across the team
- Enable rapid and secure incident response

### Typical Communication
- Weekly security reviews with PM and Product Lead
- Security assessment reports and recommendations
- Code review comments on security concerns
- Incident response coordination and postmortems

---

## Operations/DevOps

### Role Summary
Operations/DevOps Engineers maintain CI/CD pipelines, monitor system health, optimize deployments, and ensure reliability. They enable the team to deploy confidently and respond quickly to production issues.

### Responsibilities
- Build and maintain CI/CD pipelines and automation
- Monitor system health, performance, and availability
- Manage infrastructure and deployment environments
- Optimize deployment processes and reduce cycle time
- Respond to incidents and coordinate incident resolution
- Collaborate with Developers on build and deployment troubleshooting
- Work with Security Specialists on secure deployments and compliance

### Goals
- Maximize system uptime and reliability
- Reduce deployment time and increase deployment frequency
- Automate operational tasks and reduce toil
- Enable fast incident detection and recovery

### Typical Communication
- Release planning sessions with PMs and Developers
- System health reports and incident retrospectives
- Infrastructure change notifications and runbooks
- On-call handoffs and incident updates
